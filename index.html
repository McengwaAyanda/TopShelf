About Us

Spirit Revelation Ecclesia is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spiritual growth, fellowship, and serving our community. Founded by Pastor John Asike, our church is committed to revealing God's love and power through worship, teaching, and outreach.

Our mission is to inspire and equip individuals to live out their faith, fostering a deeper connection with God and meaningful relationships with others.
